How to play

  1. You have 60 seconds to answer as many questions as possible.
  2. Correct answers earn 100 points plus a speed bonus.
  3. Consecutive correct answers build a score multiplier.
  4. A wrong answer resets your streak. Passing moves to the next question.
  5. Use buttons, number keys, or the microphone where browser support is available.
